Police Shootings in 2015

In 2015, over 1,500 people were shot and killed by police officers in the US. This page visualizes the 2015 police shooting data from the Washington Post from three main attributes: 1) Race, 2) Flee Status and 3) Age.

By Race

This bar chart shows the distribution of people who were shot by the police by race in 2015.

By Flee Status

This ring chart shows the proportion of people shot by the police who tried to flee. Note the gaps in between each arc for ease of distinguishing shapes and the area the shape contains.

By Age

This histogram chart shows the distribution of the age of the people who were shot by the police in 2015.
